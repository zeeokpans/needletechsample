Why Needle Technology?

Needle Technology is an Innovative Business Optimization Consulting firm that uses technology as a tool to simplify operational processes for our clients. Our services are tailored specifically based on our clients requirements. Chances are we have the right cost-efficiency offerings for your business, thus making us the right IT partner. Additionally,

➾Our solutions are not only built with powerful design tools, they are also built with a scalability approach, offering clients opportunity to reduce operational expenses and control spending.

➾Our philosophy is to offer qualitative value added services with a minimal budgetary allocation from initial stage to implementation stage.

➾Our services are geared towards providing innovation and improving the business models of our clients.

➾Our transition methodology allows clients to quickly scale up and manage complex processes easily.

➾Needle Technology has a dedicated process excellence team that uses Six Sigma, Lean and COPC frameworks for continuous improvements in operations that deliver benefits with every engagement.

➾Our clientele covers government MDA’s, multinational corporations, and Small &Medium Enterprises.

➾ We enable our clients to turn their ideas and vision into results, improving their business operations using IT tools and solutions.

➾ Our knowledge base covers key functions such as systems assessment, qualitative service delivery, effectiveness, and costing vs. budgeting of delivering IT project services.
